About

Who is writing this blog?

Hi, I'm kittykat. Right now, I'm learning how to make web apps using clojure and clojurescript. My background is in applied math and stats, so I have a little experience using Matlab and R, if-statements, functions, that sort of thing. But I want to make web apps now, and I started the year (2021) with no idea how. I literally had to look up the difference between frontend and backend.

What is the purpose of this blog?

Learning how to do fullstack web development has been an overwhelming process for me. Using Clojure and Clojurescript has helped simplify things, and there are many resources on these subjects. But I would like to refer to a single resource most of the time. So I'm compiling my notes here as I figure things out and I'm making them public in case they might be helpful to anyone else.

I highly encourage comments and questions. They will help me learn and they will be helpful to other readers.

Ttyl! :)
